Privacy Policy

AtmosMix · Last updated: 3 June 2026

AtmosMix ("the app") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

AtmosMix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying details.

Data Stored on Your Device

Any mixes you create and save, along with your settings, are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, this data is removed.

Third-Party Services

AtmosMix does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

AtmosMix does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
